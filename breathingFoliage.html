Breathing Foliage

For people who spend most of time indoor, such as offices or family houses, the pollution of interior air is an important problem that easier to neglect. This product uses a more friendly way to show the level of inside air quality, remaiding us to ventilate in time.

How it works

  • When no one in front, the foliage swings slowly. If the infrared sensor detects somebody passing by, the foliage will shake quickly like saying hello.
  • When the air quality is high, the LED lights inside foliage are green. When the air quality goes bad, the green LED lights turn to red and dark.
  • If the foliage are red or dark, the foliage will ask people to ventilate when some one passing by.
