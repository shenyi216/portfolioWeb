Petrouchka

Petrouchka is an experiment of children storytelling system using mutiple touch devices and displays. Children can use tablets or smart phones to control characters, moving them and calling their default motions by gestures to tell story on the screen. The aim of this experiment is to explore a creative communication platform for children in this "Touch-Screen Era".
By the way, the name "Petrouchka" stems from a famous Russian Ballet.

Points

  • Combination between mobile touch devices and display
  • Use gestures to call default motions.
  • Multiple users cooperation.

Created with

  • Unity 3D
  • TUIO protocal
  • $1 gesture recognition arithmetic
